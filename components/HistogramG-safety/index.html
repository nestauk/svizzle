Safety

You can adjust the safety distances to control overflow using:

- `safetyXNoTicks`: the amount of pixels from the axis to the border when you use `hideTicks`.

- `safetyXTicks`: the amount of pixels from the axis to the border when you show ticks.

- `safetyXValues`: the amounf of pixels from top of the taller bin to the opposite border.

- `safetyY`: the number of pixels on top and bottom limits.
